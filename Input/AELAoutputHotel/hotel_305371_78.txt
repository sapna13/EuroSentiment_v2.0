It is in great location (just above Pacific Place at Admiralty MTR station) and the room is more than just comfortable, but elegant and luxurious. Room service is reasonably nice. I haven't tried out the gym but the pool is quite nice. Having stayed at Shangri la next door and Regal in Causeway Bay previously, I would definately prefer this one and will come back again when I visit family  friends in HK.
