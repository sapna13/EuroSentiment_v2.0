Strange.The hotel's biggest drawback, in my opinion, is its location. Nusa Dua is quiet and luxurious, but too far from anything else in Bali. To get to another beach, dive site, restaurant, or activity you need a 20-30 minute taxi ride. That makes the hotel good for only sitting by the pool\/beach and using only its restaurants, which for Bali are overpriced.Overall the Laguna was a nice experience, but would have been better in a different part of the island.
