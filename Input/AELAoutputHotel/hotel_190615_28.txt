The only minor problems we found were the room we had was on the top level under the roof top restaurant\/bar so we heard the movement of tables and people till late in the night. The last day\/night we were there our room had a very bad smell of sewer. Although we let the front desk know about it early in the day they did nothing about it till we couldnt take it any longer about 1am and then they let us use another room to sleep in that last night.Over all the hotel is in an EXCELLENT location to all the major streets for shopping and dining.
